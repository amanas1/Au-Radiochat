Au Radio Chat Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-24

Overview

Au Radio Chat is a global radio streaming app that helps users discover and listen to live radio stations from around the world.

Data We May Process

The app may process basic app usage information, radio preferences, favorites, and optional profile details entered by the user.

Permissions

Camera and microphone permissions are only intended for user-triggered features that require them. Internet access is required for radio streaming and app content delivery.

Local Storage

Some settings, favorites, and profile preferences may be stored locally on the device to improve the listening experience.

Third-Party Streams

Radio content may be delivered from third-party radio stations and stream providers. Au Radio Chat does not claim ownership of third-party audio content.
