# Content Rating Draft

Recommended questionnaire direction for the current build:

- Violence: `No`
- Sexual content: `No`
- Profanity: `No`
- Drugs / alcohol / tobacco: `No`
- Gambling: `No`
- User-generated content: `Yes`
- User interaction: `Yes`
- Shares location: `No`
- Digital purchases: `No`

Because the app includes optional chat/user interaction, expect a rating that is higher than a pure radio player.
