## Store Listing Strategy
- Put `live radio` and `world radio` in title/short description.
- Put `sleep timer`, `equalizer`, `favorites`, `genre discovery`, `radio stations worldwide` in full description.
- Use screenshot captions with search-intent phrases, not abstract slogans.
- Repeat core keywords naturally 2-4 times in the long description, without spam.

## Screenshot Caption Set
1. `Listen to live radio stations worldwide`
2. `Find music by genre, mood, and era`
3. `Save favorite stations and switch fast`
4. `Tune sound with EQ and immersive effects`
5. `Use sleep timer for night listening`
6. `Enjoy a premium visual radio experience`
